About

A place for ideas worth keeping.

I’m Kazi, a software engineer in the San Francisco Bay Area. My professional interests center on infrastructure, distributed systems, security, and artificial intelligence.

Outside of engineering, I spend a lot of time following questions through history, philosophy, political economy, and Islam. This site is where I turn those rabbit holes into something more durable.

I’m less interested in performing certainty than in becoming clearer over time.
